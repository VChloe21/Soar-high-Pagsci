Core Values
``CORE VALUES``

Core values in a school are the guiding principles that shape how students, teachers, and staff behave, learn, and treat one another. They help define the school’s character and purpose.

Here’s what they do:

1. Guide behavior
– They remind everyone how to act with respect, honesty, and responsibility.
2. Build character
– They help students develop good morals and positive attitudes.
3. Create unity
– They bring the school community together with shared beliefs and goals.
4. Support learning
– They inspire students to do their best, stay disciplined, and care about others.
Shape the school’s identity
– They make the school stand out and show what it stands for.
